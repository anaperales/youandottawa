Welcome to You & Ottawa

This interactive map connects Ottawa’s waterways with local sustainability action.

How to Use This Map

  • Search: Type a name or topic (e.g., "Thrift," "Coffee") to find specific hidden gems.
  • Explore: Click any icon on the map to see the story and details.
  • Filter: Click the Filter tool (funnel icon) to see specific categories like Dining or Thrift.
  • Navigate: Zoom in on the map to find spots near you!

About the Project

Created by Ana Perales as part of the Waterlution Y4RC Cohort. This guide challenges the idea that there is "nothing to do" in Ottawa by making the invisible water footprint visible through local action.
