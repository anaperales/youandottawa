The Context

Ottawa lives and breathes by the water.

Did you know our drinking water travels through 3,000km of pipes before returning to the watershed?

Every bite we eat and item we buy connects us to this cycle.


This isn't just a map, it's an invitation to join the movement.

Use the guide below to find student-friendly events, sustainable shops, and projects where you can take real action.
